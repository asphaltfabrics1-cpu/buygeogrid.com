1. Download each image from your Squarespace site
2. Rename them according to the names above (lowercase, hyphens instead of spaces)
3. Place them in the correct subdirectory as shown above

## After Placing Images

Once all images are in place, your site will display them correctly. The dev server will automatically pick up the changes.

You can verify images are working by:
1. Opening http://localhost:3002 in your browser
2. Checking that all logos and product images display correctly
3. Opening the browser console (F12) to check for any 404 errors
